Notes & assumptions:
  • Origin storage is excluded — this comparison assumes the container is only stored at the destination (e.g. loaded, transported, and held before delivery), not rented at origin first.
  • Packrat waives the first 30 days of destination storage, so only months beyond the first are billed at $446.25/mo.
  • PODS does not waive its first month — delivery, the lock, and the first 30 days of storage are billed upfront as one fixed charge, whether or not the full 30 days is actually used.
  • PODS' 20%-off monthly rate ($409.00 → $327.20) only applies when total storage is 2 months or longer. At exactly 1 month, no discount applies and the first-month charge uses the full $409.00 list price ($435.45 total instead of $353.65). Once storage reaches 2+ months, the discount applies to the first month and every additional month.
  • Packrat's destination monthly total already includes tax, per the quote. Transportation is a flat one-time estimate, applied once regardless of storage duration.
  • Packrat includes optional movers at origin at no charge — a $603.69 value, shown as included rather than added to the total.
  • PODS transportation (fuel surcharge + long-distance transit) is a one-time charge, applied once regardless of storage duration; this discount isn't tied to the storage-duration condition above.
  • Figures are estimates from the quotes provided and may not include every possible fee (e.g. insurance tiers, extra mileage, warehouse storage instead of at-residence).
